Rules

There are two players in Blackjack, you and the dealer (computer).

Your goal is to beat the dealer by having a higher point score. Blackjack cards are scored by their point total. The person with the highest total score, that isnâ€™t above 21, wins the round. A point score higher than 21 is a loss. The cards 2 through 10 are worth their number value, and the face cards (jack, queen, king) are all worth 10. An ace is worth 11, unless this creates a total score higher than 21, in which case it is worth 1.

You have two options as you play: hit or stand.

Hit: You take another card, and its value gets added onto your score
Stand: You take no more cards and your current score is your final score

Try to beat the dealer as many times as you can!
